Trust & Safety

Tenant and Landlord Safety Rules

Tenants

  • View the property before paying deposits.
  • Confirm the landlord is verified where possible.
  • Do not share passwords, ID numbers, or banking OTPs in chat.
  • Report suspicious listings immediately.

Landlords

  • Upload accurate photos and prices.
  • Do not request illegal fees or discriminate against applicants.
  • Keep verification documents current.
  • Respond to tenants professionally.
