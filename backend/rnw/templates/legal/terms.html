RNW Terms and Conditions

Version 2026-06. This template is provided for launch readiness and should be reviewed by a South African legal professional before public release.

1. Platform role

RNW helps tenants find rooms and rentals near work and helps landlords advertise available accommodation. RNW is a marketplace and does not own, inspect, or guarantee every property unless explicitly stated.

2. Accounts

Users must provide accurate information, keep login details secure, and use the platform lawfully. RNW may suspend accounts involved in fraud, abusive conduct, fake listings, or payment abuse.

3. Subscriptions

Tenant Plus is R50 per month and Landlord Pro is R100 per month. Subscription access may depend on successful payment confirmation from the payment provider.

4. Listings and applications

Landlords are responsible for accurate listing details, lawful rental terms, and proof of authority to rent the property. Tenants are responsible for submitting truthful application information.

5. Safety

Users should not pay deposits outside safe channels until they have verified the property and landlord. Report suspicious listings immediately.

6. Liability

RNW is not liable for offline agreements, direct payments, property condition, or disputes between tenants and landlords except where required by law.
