Refund and Cancellation Policy

Tenant Plus costs R50 per month and Landlord Pro costs R100 per month. Users can cancel future renewals from the billing dashboard.

Refunds may be considered for duplicate charges, confirmed technical billing errors, or platform access failures. RNW may decline refunds where the subscription benefit was used or where abuse is suspected.

Rental deposits, rent, and offline payments between tenants and landlords are not RNW subscription payments and are not refunded by RNW.
